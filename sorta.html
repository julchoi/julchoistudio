Sorta · UX/UI 2025

AI File Organizer for Music Producers

By automatically detecting tempo, key, and mood, Sorta organizes music files like demos, loops, and beats into smart folders. The project focuses on simplifying creative workflow so artists can spend less time searching, and more time making music.
