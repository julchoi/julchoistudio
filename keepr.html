  • [instructor] Sally Chung

Keepr is an AI music organizer designed to help producers and musicians manage their growing sound libraries effortlessly. By automatically detecting tempo, key, and mood, it organizes demos, loops, and beats into smart folders. The project focuses on simplifying creative workflow so artists can spend less time searching, and more time making music.
