MORII means the desire to capture the fleeting moment. It's a time you least expected, a time when that moment spontaneously confronts you, but there is nothing you can do to preserve it. For me, that moment is when I see something beautiful. So, I created this magazine, MORII, which features everything you want to remember regarding interior and furniture design.
